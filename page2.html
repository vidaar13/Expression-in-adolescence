Strategies to cope with changes

Back

There are many changes that occur in adolescence, a stage that all of us have just started. These changes can be scary and unpredictable. With them comes the need to express ourselves. There are ways to express ourselves that everyone may not understand. It's frustrating. We will save ourselves that frustration..

Alongside the ways of expression people can’t understand, there are ways of expression people can. Using our strengths and hobbies, we will meet our need for expression with a choice. We will provide some options, but it’s really up to you to decide. Here are the top 5 ways to express yourself:

1. Talking - 52.5% of people

"I talk to whoever I have a problem with, and try to solve the problem, by expressing emotions, too."

2. Music - 51.2% of people

3. Physical activity - 50% of people

"I try playing my best and having fun"

4. Playing with peers - 40% of people

5.Visual Art - 33.8% of people

"Visual Arts because, art is an really effective way of expressing myself."
